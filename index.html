Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

WebTools

WebTools does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

All features (GUID Finder, Broken Link Checker, Bulk Link Opener, Color Picker, and Text Converter) operate entirely on the user’s local device. No browsing history, personal identifiers, or sensitive data are collected or transmitted.

UltraBoost – Video & Audio Enhancer

UltraBoost also does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The extension only interacts with video elements on web pages to apply user-selected zoom/centering and optional audio volume boost. It stores user preferences (enabled state, zoom level, mode, and volume boost) locally using Chrome Sync, so settings can be remembered across devices.

No browsing history, personal identifiers, or video content are collected or transmitted. UltraBoost does not sell, trade, or transfer any user information to third parties.

General

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer user information to third parties for any purpose. All extensions operate locally in the browser and respect your privacy.
